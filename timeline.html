Our Journey: 6 Pillars of Love 💖

Har pal khaas hai, par yeh woh 6 pal hain jinhone humare rishte ko mazboot banaya.

#1 The First Sight 💘

The day everything changed

Jab hum pehli baar mile, meri *heartbeat* tez ho gayi thi (jo aaj bhi hoti hai)। Ussi pal maine apni rooh aapke hawaale kar di। Aapko dekh kar jaan aati hai, jiii...

#2 The Anchor of Life 🔗

The realization

Pehli baar jab aapko dekha, toh dekhte hi laga ki *yehi woh hain* jinke saath hamesha rehna hai। Bas yehi meri *zindagi* hai aur yehi mera *sab kuch* hai!

#3 The Pillar of Strength 🛡️

A time of challenge

Jis din mujhe *brain tumor* ka pata chala, aapka 'Main aapke saath hoon' kehna meri sabse badi *himmat* bani। Ab mujhe jeena bhi bas *aapke liye* hai।

#4 The Crazy Fun Moment 😂

The day we broke the rules

Woh pehli baar ki *patties! Jab tissue nahi mila toh aapne bhi mere jaise **apne kapdon se haath ponch liye* the। Uss pal samjha ki aap mere jaise hi *simple aur crazy* ho!

#5 The Future Vibe 💍

The ultimate promise

Future mein aur kuch nahi chahiye, bas *aapka saath! Aage jo bhi mushkil hogi, main sab kuch dekh lunga, **sab ko mana lunga*, bas aap mere saath rehna।

#6 The First Embrace 🤗

Pure, true love

Mera pyaar aapke liye *humesha bina shart* ke hi raha hai। Aur jab hum *pehli baar ek hue (first hug), tab mehsoos hua ki yeh sirf pyaar nahi, yeh meri **duniya* hai। Woh pal aaj bhi mere dil mein *tham sa gaya* hai।
